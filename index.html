Trees Grow in Brooklyn

Hello, I'm Leah! I am working on my tree identification skills. Please read on for pictures and information about some trees I (think) I have identified correctly in Brooklyn. All images were taken by me!

New York City is home to many beautiful trees that do a lot of work to keep our city healthy! In addition to being beautiful, NYC's trees:

  1. divert stormwater,
  2. remove air pollutants,
  3. and conserve energy.

See the table below for more information:

Tree Species Stormwater Intercepted (gallons/year) Energy Conserved (kWh/year) Air Pollutants Removed (pounds/year)
Flowering Dogwood 1,044,922 1,118,984 1,973
Kanzan Cherry 12,598,405 13,692,951 24,007
